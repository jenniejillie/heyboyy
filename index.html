Important Facts about Plastic Waste

Did You Know?

  • Over 8 million tons of plastic waste end up in our oceans every year.
  • Plastic takes up to 1,000 years to decompose in landfills.
  • Less than 10% of plastic used globally is recycled.
  • Plastic pollution is harming wildlife, with animals ingesting or becoming entangled in plastic waste.
  • Every minute, one garbage truck of plastic is dumped into our oceans.
